About Me

I am a highly motivated Sophmore in College at Elon University, currently double majoring in Computer Science and English with a Concentration in Creative Writing with a minor in Game Design. I'm interested in each of these areas, as seperately they are their own unique puzzles to give my brain the stimulation and engagement it needs. Together, in video games, it's like a never ending puzzle for me to solve, and I love video games for providing me this never-ending stimulation and activity. If I ever get bored or tired of one area, I can try to switch to another or focus on another. Similarly, I can use each field as a hobby while I'm focusing on one as my occupation.

Why is my portfolio structured in this way

My portfolio is structured in this way to make navigation and editing my portfolio(s) as easy as possible for myself. When I have multiple things in lots of different places, I tend to struggle to get things done. To make finding things easy, I will be breaking down what is on each portfolio at the top of each page.
